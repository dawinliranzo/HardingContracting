About Harding Contracting

Founded by Angel Serano, Harding Contracting Inc has been serving West Harrison and the greater New York area with pride and dedication. We believe that quality construction is built on trust, transparency, and craftsmanship.

Every project we undertake—whether a small repair or a full-scale build—is approached with the same level of commitment. We use only premium materials, employ skilled tradespeople, and maintain open communication throughout the entire process.

Our reputation is built on referrals from satisfied clients who know they can count on us to deliver exceptional results, every time.
